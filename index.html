A week after George Floyd’s murder hundreds of videos have been released documenting abuse of power and excessive force by police against peaceful protesters. Many of our representatives who are elected to represent us are silent on this issue and not using their platforms for change.

This website has data on over 100 videos of police brutality occurring at protests nationwide, and the information of local city councilors and the mayor of the cities in which it occurred.
